STRATEGY & POSITIONING

OUR CREATIVE PROCESS

1. Discovery

Define the proplem (Rasearch and Insight):

Who does it affect? How does it affect them? How does it make them feel?

Understand the audience (Insight):

Demographic cohorts - likes/
dislikes, cultural trends
Physchometric profiling

2. Exploration

Research:

Existing brand collateral
Market trends and competitor analysis
Cultural crossovers - relevant films, music, art

Brainstorm initial ideas

3. Conceptualise

Outline the story and golden thread.
Ideate and sketch initial solutions across different media

4. Creation

Work up polished concepts

Test concepts with client and sample audience (using Vibe technology where appropriate).

5. production & Delivery

Work up final solution in all its forms

Deliver communications to audience(s).
