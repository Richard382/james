We devote our talent and technology to strengthen relationships and enhance performance.

Welcome to a world of better relationships

At Laws of Attraction, we believe better businesses start with stronger relationships. That’s why we provide a practical and truly human-centric approach to measuring and managing an organisation‘s biggest opportunity and risk – its people.

Everything we do is centred around enhancing relationships in order to increase employee engagement, develop people, and improve business results. At the heart of this is Vibe® – our award-winning survey and feedback platform, which provides deep, actionable insights into every aspect of organisational culture.

OUR VALUES

Our values framework serves as the compass that guides our collective actions, behaviours and
decsion-making process. In essence they are simultaneously the rules that guide us and the ties that bind us.

LISTEN & LEARN

Good listeners are fast learners, more productive, better problem-solvers and more likely to be respected and trusted. What’s not to love?

ACTION & ACCOUNTABILITY

To stop moving is to go backwards, especially in tech, which is why we all take accountability for our actions and endeavour to keep things moving.

LOYALTY & LOVE

We inspire loyalty by trusting our people to self-manage. This creates stability (not to mention fantastic work) and from there the love grows.

CREATIVITY & COMMERCIALITY

We do more than simply dream up creative ideas, we pioneer strategies that are aligned and synchronised with commercial ambitions.

ENERGY & EMPATHY

Empathy inspires transformative energy by uniting individuals and aligning organisations in the pursuit of shared goals and objectives.

OPEN & INCLUSIVE
